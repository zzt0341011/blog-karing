## What Is Karing VPN?

[Karing](https://github.com/KaringX/karing) is a VPN application built on the Clash core, with support for **Windows, macOS and Android**. It lets you change the IP address you use to access the internet. For example, if [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/) is not available in your country, Karing can help you reach it. Karing is similar to tools such as V2rayNG, [Sing-Box](https://sing-box.info/zh), Clash for Windows, [Clash.Meta](https://github.com/MetaCubeX/ClashMetaForAndroid), [Hiddify](https://hiddify.me/zh), [V2Box](https://v2box.pro/zh), [Shadowrocket](https://shadowrocket.ink/zh), [Stash](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/stash-rule-based-proxy/id1596063349), [Surge](https://github.com/Jounce/Surge), [mahsang](https://mahsang.pro) and [nekobox](https://nekobox.info/zh), but it adds many new features, helps hide your identity online, and is faster than a traditional **VPN**. If you need a more stable and secure network, we recommend using paid proxy nodes. Karing supports almost every proxy protocol, including Shadowsocks, VMess, VLESS, Reality, Trojan and Hysteria2.

## How to Use Karing VPN

- Pick an affordable proxy service (airport).
- A plan with no time limit is best — for example, [NetExpress](https://1.jnk.ink/ad2RVl).
- Open the homepage, then enter your email and password to register.

![Karing airport registration page with email and password fields](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1001.jpg)

- After registering, log in.
- Click the store and purchase a subscription.

![Logging in and opening the subscription store](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1002.jpg)

- You will see several plans. Find the ¥7 / 20 GB plan with no time limit.
- Pay by scanning the QR code with Alipay.

![Choosing the 20 GB plan and paying with Alipay QR code](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1003.jpg)

- After payment, click the tutorial in the top-left corner.
- Then click "Universal Subscription" and copy the general subscription link.

![Copying the universal subscription link from the tutorial page](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1004.jpg)

- Open the app, tap "Add Profile".
- Then tap "Add Profile from Link".

![Adding a new profile from a subscription link in Karing](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1005.jpg)

- Paste the subscription link into the blank field, then tap the check mark in the top-right corner to save.

![Pasting the subscription link and saving the profile](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1006.jpg)

- Once saved, tap the red button to turn on the proxy.

![Turning on the Karing proxy with the red power button](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1007.jpg)

- Tap the arrow in the bottom-right corner to choose a server.

![Selecting a proxy server node in Karing](https://karing.biz/img/nongfu1008.jpg)
